Service communities enable cross-government collaboration by making it easier for people to work together on an end-to-end service.

Join a service community

You might be interested in service communities if the end-to-end service that you work on is delivered by two or more departments.

Most service communities are facilitated by people who start them. There are 4 active service communities. Learn more about how to join one.

They are open to people in all roles.

Run a service community

Service communities are started and facilitated by people who work on an end-to-end service which is delivered by two or more departments.

If you think you need a service community which does not exist yet, GDS can help you get the scope right and provide resources on how to run it. Learn more about how to run a service community.
