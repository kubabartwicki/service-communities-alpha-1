Being in a service community makes it easier for people to collaborate across government on an end-to-end service.

Join a service community

There are 4 active service communities:

  • Start a business
  • Employ someone
  • Import-Export
  • Get health benefits

They meet regularly, work together to improve an end-to-end service and are open to people in all roles. Learn more about how to join a service community.

Run a service community

Anyone can start and run a service community. The service communities team can help you get the scope right and provide resources on how to run it. Learn more about how to run a service community.
