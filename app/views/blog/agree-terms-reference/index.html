Agree terms of reference

Posted by: Kuba Bartwicki, Posted on: 1 December 2018
{% include "contributions.html" %}

A terms of reference document was originally drafted in the start a business community, but has since been used in a number of communities. It is used by members to give to line managers and directors to get buy in for the community. It’s a document that helps legitimise and unify the community and it’s way of working.

Members of the start a business drafted a terms of reference. In it, they specified the principles that a service community will adhere to. Such as creating a user centred focus across departments.

It detailed the roles and responsibilities within the service community, including who the facilitator and sponsors.
Further to this, it outlined the logistics such as when meetings will take place, and how the community will share information and resources.

A draft of the terms of reference was then shared with all of the start a business community to comment and iterate on.
