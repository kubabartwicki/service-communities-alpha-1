Agree terms of reference

Posted by: Kuba Bartwicki, Posted on: 1 December 2018
{% include "contributions.html" %}

A terms of reference document was originally drafted in the start a business community, but has since been used in a number of communities. It’s a document that helps legitimises the community and it’s way of working, making it easier to for senior stakeholders to get bought into community work.

In the the start a business service community terms of reference it specified the principles that a service community will adhere to. Such as creating a user centred focus across departments.

It detailed the roles and responsibilities within the service community, including appointing a facilitator and sponsors.
Further to this, it outlined the logistics such as when meetings will take place, and how the community will share information and resources.

A draft of the terms of reference was then shared with all of the start a business community to comment and iterate on.

By creating a terms of reference the start a business community agreed how they would work together. Agreeing the terms of refrences demonstrates memebers committment to the service community and it's way of working.

Contact Kuba if have any questions about how to use this in your service community.
