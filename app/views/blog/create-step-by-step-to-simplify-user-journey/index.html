Create a step-by-step to help simplify the user journey on GOV.UK

Posted by: Will Harmer, Posted on: 21 October 2018
{% include "contributions.html" %}

A step by step navigation is a way of presenting end-to-end journeys. It groups existing guidance and transactions into a series of steps. This blog post explains more.

Many step by step navigations have now been created, including Learning to drive and Employing someone. The start a business service community used a workshop to identify potential step by steps, ranging in scope from 'setup a business' to 'choose legal entity'. They worked with the GDS GOV.UK team to plan potential step by steps and then used a further workshop to review and create prototypes.

Contact Will if have any questions about how to use this in your service community.
