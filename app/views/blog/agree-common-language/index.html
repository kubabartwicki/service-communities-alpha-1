Agree a common language

Posted by: Kuba Bartwicki, Posted on: 15 November 2018
{% include "contributions.html" %}

The Start A Business service community created a Common Terms document, defining exactly what is meant by certain words and phrases.

They used part of a broader workshop to identify 15 key terms of which service community members had varying understandings. These included “company”, “sole trader” and “validation”. Members agreed and recorded a single definition for each.

Using this has helped service community members to avoid misunderstandings and confusion.

Contact Kuba if have any questions about how to use this in your service community.
