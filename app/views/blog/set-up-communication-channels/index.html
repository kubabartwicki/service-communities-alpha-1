Tips for setting up communication channels

Posted by: Will Harmer, Posted on: 28 November 2018
{% include "contributions.html" %}

It’s important that everyone in the Service Community can easily communicate with each other, and don’t have to wait for meetings or conference calls. This will help with information sharing, unblocking problems and ensuring cohesion among members. There are many ways to keep people connected:

  • set up a Google Group, which acts as an online forum and email list
  • create a dedicated Slack for the community
  • have a shared online calendar
  • arrange frequent short meetings (sometimes called stand-ups) that everyone dials into to share their current work and blockers

Contact Will if have any questions about how to use this in your service community.
